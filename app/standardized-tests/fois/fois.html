TUBE INDEPENDENT (levels 1-3)

  1. 1. No oral intake
  2. 2. Tube dependent with minimal/inconsistent oral intake
  3. 3. Tube supplements with consistent oral intake

TOTAL ORAL INTAKE (levels 4-7)

  1. 4. Total oral intake of a single consistency
  2. 5. Total oral intake of multiple consistencies requiring special preparation
  3. 6. Total oral intake with no special preparation, but must avoid specific foods or liquid items
  4. 7. Total oral intake with no restrictions
